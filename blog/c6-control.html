When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?
The main causes of triggers can be poor direction, or an inability to understand what is expected to be delivered. In essence managing expectation and being able to envisage how to deliver the result. Disproportionate emotional frustration comes from hitting walls when coding, examples of which comes from getting to the point in software development where by you get frustrated with the result you are attempting to acheive is illusive.

How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?
SBNRR or the Siberian North Railroad is a menthod of capturing negative emotions, understanding their source and attempting to in a measured way understand the source of said emotions. In this way we can catch the point or source of the emotions and using breathing exercises calm the mind unitl the moment has passed. The Siberian North Railroad acronym comes from the concept of going into the cold and cooling the mind.
