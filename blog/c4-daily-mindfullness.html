What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?
From my perspective, I found it relatively easy to gain and keep rapport for using this process. Gaining agreement and assuring the person become more natural in the flow of conversation and bring about positive and constructive tones when communicating.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?
Of the three Mindful conversation, Listening and Dipping. I felt that Dipping would be the most relative to me. You can listen to others, but you have also to listen to yourself and understand where you are.
