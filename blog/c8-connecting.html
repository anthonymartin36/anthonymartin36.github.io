Have you tried using any of the insight and techniques for growing trust and sincerity? How did it go?
The Book outlines in this chapter that Trust is very important. Weather it be in a team or one on one coaching/mentoring. The latter point about sincerity can some down to two things, according to Dr. Karen May: 1)Practivly giveing people the benifit of the Doubt, and 2) Remember trust begets Trust. Aristotle said, “It is the mark of an educated mind to be able to entertain a thought without accepting it.”

Try empathic listening - how did it go?
Before reading this chapter, I attempted empathic listening. I did this with sales appointments I had. Effectively I had to give my undivided attention in those meetings as any relative infomation is gold. I would do this by sitting up straight and breathing through my nose straight into my stomach. This lowered my heart rate and allowed me to take in all the relative infomration. It also had the effect of removing any perspective that would have clouded my judgement. I found this to be a very good tool.

How did you find the loving kindness meditation?
I reflected on a Family member that I had not though of for some time. It allowed me to see the world from their perspective, which I can't recall the last time I gave it some thought. It was good.
